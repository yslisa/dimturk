Instructions

Welcome and thank you for volunteering! In this experiment you will see displays like this one:

Each object in these displays differ in:
1. Color (green, red or yellow)
2. Shape (square, circle or triangle)
3. Texture (plaid, dots or waves)

On each trial of the experiment, you will choose one of the three objects and collect points points based on your choice. After each choice, you may receive either 1 or 0 points. At the end of the experiment a bonus will be calculated according to how many points you have earned in total.

In each trial, you can choose the left, middle or right object by pressing the keys ''b'', ''n'', or ''m'' respectively.

There is some rule, but we won't tell you.

When you're ready to continue, press Next.
